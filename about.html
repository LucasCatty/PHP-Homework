About WMTS Football Team

Welcome to the official page of WMTSS Football Team. We are a passionate group of players, staff, and supporters who believe in the power of football to unite and inspire our community.

Founded in 2010, WMTSS has grown from a small group of players to a well-respected team, participating in national and regional tournaments. Our mission is to promote teamwork, discipline, and sportsmanship among the youth.

We invite everyone to join us on our journey as we continue to make strides in both the sports arena and community development.
